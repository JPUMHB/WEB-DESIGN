My academic journey has always been important to me, culminating in a 4.2 GPA upon graduating high school. Growing up, I was deeply involved in sports, proudly participating in both football and basketball. Though I have fond memories of my time on the field, once I got a little older my true athletic passion began to lie with basketball, a sport I continue to dedicate myself to.

Out of highschool I was thrilled to have committed to Regis University to play basketball, marking an exciting step in my athletic journey. Simultaneously, my lifelong love for technology has driven me to pursue a degree in computer science. This academic focus allows me to delve into a field I find incredibly engaging. A particularly fond memory of mine is traveling to Greece, an experience that instilled in me a deep appreciation for travel, a hobby I look forward to pursuing further in the years to come.

My athletic journey, particularly in basketball, has been a profound teacher, introducing to me invaluable lessons in patience, perseverance, and resilience. Navigating injuries, conflicting schedules, and even moments where others' intentions weren't aligned with my own, has forged a deeper understanding of these qualities. Through these experiences, I've learned, and continue to learn, the importance of having faith and trusting the process, both on and off the court.
